# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make our community welcoming, safe, and equitable for all. We are committed to fostering an environment that respects the dignity, rights, and contributions of all individuals, regardless of race, ethnicity, caste, color, age, disability, sex or gender, gender identity or expression, sexual orientation, neurodiversity, language, religion, national or social origin, socio-economic status, level of education, or other status.

## Encouraged Behaviors

- Showing empathy and kindness toward other contributors
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Taking responsibility for mistakes and learning from them
- Focusing on what is best for the overall community

## Restricted Behaviors

- Harassment, trolling, and personal or political attacks
- Sexualized language, imagery, or unwanted advances of any kind
- Publishing someone's private information without explicit consent
- Conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. They may remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct and will communicate reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate.

## Reporting an Issue

Instances of unacceptable behavior can be reported by opening a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/openfoodfacts/estore-extension/issues) or contacting a maintainer directly. All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled confidentially. Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter.

## Addressing and Repairing Harm

Maintainers will use the following guidelines when responding to violations:

1. **Warning** - A private written warning explaining the violation. A written apology may be requested.
2. **Temporarily Limited Activities** - A time-limited cooldown from specific channels or interactions. Further violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban.
3. **Temporary Suspension** - A suspension from all community spaces with conditions for return. No public or private interaction with those involved is permitted during this period.
4. **Permanent Ban** - Permanent removal from all community spaces, reserved for repeated serious violations or behavior so harmful that no other remedy is appropriate.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, and also applies when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces. Examples include using an official email address, posting via an official social media account, or acting as an appointed representative at an online or offline event.
